I am a PhD candidate in Statistical Cosmology at the <a href="http://www.iap.fr/?langue=en">Institut d'Astrophysique de Paris</a> and a member of the <a href="https://www.aquila-consortium.org">Aquila</a> and <a href="https://www.esa.int/Science_Exploration/Space_Science/Euclid">Euclid</a> consortia. I also serve as a teaching assistant in Mathematics at <a href="https://www.sorbonne-universite.fr/en">Sorbonne Université</a>.

My current research centres on developing novel methods to constrain the equation of state of dark energy and the sum of neutrino masses, using non-standard probes of the cosmic web.
