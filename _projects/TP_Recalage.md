## What is image registration?

Image registration is the process of aligning two images. This is a very important step in many applications, such as medical imaging, where it is used to align different scans of the same patient, or in remote sensing, where it is used to align images taken by different satellites.

## In a nutshell

In this practical (in French), we propose a very simple model of image registration. The goal is to find a "good" transformation $$T$$ such that $$T(I_1) \approx I_2$$.
