Tristan Hoellinger

Institut d'Astrophysique de Paris — CNRS — Sorbonne Université

I am a PhD candidate in Cosmology at the Institut d'Astrophysique de Paris, and a full member of the Aquila and Euclid consortia. I additionally serve as a teaching assistant in Mathematics at Sorbonne University.

My doctoral research centres on safely enhancing cosmological simulations and inference using machine learning.

Postdoctoral availability: October 2026.


Besides my academic work, I am a passionate photographer. I meticulously craft limited-edition prints, which are available for purchase.
