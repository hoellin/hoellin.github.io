98 bis boulevard Arago

75014 Paris France

I am a PhD candidate in Cosmological Physics at the Institut d’Astrophysique de Paris. I am a full member of the Aquila and Euclid consortia, and I serve as a teaching assistant in Mathematics at Sorbonne University.

My current research centres on enhancing the robustness of cosmological simulations and inference against model misspecification.

I am exploring postdoctoral opportunities for October 2026.
